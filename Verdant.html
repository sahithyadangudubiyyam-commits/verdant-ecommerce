Our Eco Rating System

We independently rate every product from A+ to C based on materials, supply chain, end-of-life, and carbon footprint.

A+
Exceptional
Carbon-negative, 100% organic/recycled, fully circular lifecycle, regenerative impact.
A
Excellent
Carbon-neutral, predominantly sustainable materials, minimal packaging waste.
B
Good
Significant eco improvements vs. conventional. Some trade-offs noted and disclosed.
C
Improving
Transitioning toward sustainability. Better than conventional but room for growth.
